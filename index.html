Meet the Team

Susan F. Gregory, DMD

Doctor Gregory would like to welcome you to a fabulous dental experience! By combining advanced clinical dentistry with a fun, friendly style, Dr. Gregory and her team will make you and your family more comfortable than you ever thought possible.

Dr. Gregory provides some of the latest advances in dentistry—including digital x-rays, tooth-colored restorations, and in-office smile-whitening—to help you and your family maintain beautiful smiles for a lifetime. Best of all, Dr. Gregory takes the time to get to know each and every patient one-on-one, helping you make informed choices about your dental health.

Dr. Gregory earned her doctorate from the Medical University of South Carolina. She continues to study the latest dental techniques and procedures each year.

Member of the American Dental Association and the South Carolina Dental Association

Aleisha Pittman, Office Manager

Aleisha greets each of our guests as soon as they walk through our door. She does her very best to be as in-tune with your needs as she possibly can. Need help scheduling an appointment? Ask Aleisha! Need help understanding insurance benefits? Ask Aleisha! Need help working the Keurig?? Ask Aleisha! She is here to help with whatever you need and she always wears a smile.

Aleisha has been with Dr. Gregory since 2010. She is an essential member of our team and we are blessed to have her!

Aleisha was born and raised in Lugoff, South Carolina and now lives in Elgin with her husband, Brian; two sons, Lucas and Grayson; and their most recent addition, a daughter, Delaney.

Ashley Lucas, RDH, Dental Hygienist

Ashley is a very skilled and compassionate hygienist and will take her time to make you comfortable and relaxed during your appointment. She is great at patient education and can thoroughly inform you about all our procedures and dental conditions.

Ashley is from Cassatt, SC and joined our team in December 2009. She graduated from Florence-Darlington Technical College in 2005 with her Associate Degree in Dental Hygiene.

Ashley now lives in Bethune with her husband, Jimmy and son, Trevor. She will also be welcoming another son, Cohen, in December 2016.
